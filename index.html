Creative, functional, aesthetic. Design concepts are foundational principles that guide the creation of aesthetically pleasing, functional, and effective solutions. They encompass ideas like balance, contrast, alignment, and unity. These concepts help designers make deliberate choices about layout, color, typography, and imagery, ensuring their work is visually appealing and communicates its intended message clearly.
